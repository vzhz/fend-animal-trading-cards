OH LOOK A SQUIRREL!

The squirrel is my little sister's favorite animal because she feels it's energy and determination matches her own. All her loving siblings and fans enjoy sending her pictures of squirrels, especially the friendly food-grabbers! This one's for you, sibling!

  • Fave food: Dropped Oreos, preferably still in package to ensure freshness!
  • Fave passtime: Glaring at people until they drop their food
  • Fave vaycay spot: Carnival trash can
  • Aspiration: To gather a whole mountain of Oreos and sit beside them as she learns to program!

My favorite animal can have many colors or coat and shapes of ears (all beautiful!) and can live in urban or rural areas. This one in particular enjoys being with people and is coming to say hello to you! Won't you give her an Oreo?
